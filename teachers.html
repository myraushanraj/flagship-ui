Get Involved

  • Inspiration for students: By joining Flagship, teachers can inspire their students to think beyond traditional career paths and consider entrepreneurship as a viable option.
  • Networking and collaboration: Flagship provide teachers with opportunities to connect and collaborate with other educators from around the country, which can help them share best practices and learn from one another.
  • Access to resources: Flagship provides teachers with access to a wide range of resources, including lesson plans, activities, and materials, which can help them incorporate entrepreneurship education into their curriculum.
  • Opportunity to mentor: Flagship provides teachers with the opportunity to mentor and guide students as they develop their entrepreneurial skills and launch their ideas

    • Help Youth Learn More About Entrepreneurship

    Entrepreneurship education is not just about teaching students' business skills, it's about igniting a passion within them to be creative, innovative, and self-motivated learners. By integrating entrepreneurship into the curriculum, teachers can empower students to develop the skills they need to thrive in a rapidly changing world. They will learn how to think critically, work together, and make a positive impact on society. Through hands-on activities such as business plan competitions, mentorship programs, and startup incubators, students will have the opportunity to apply their knowledge to real-world situations. This not only gives them practical skills, but also a sense of purpose and the drive to make a difference in the world.
