About Us

Vision

Create a community of young, innovative, and successful entrepreneurs who are equipped with the skills and resources to help them envision & achieve their goals and make a positive impact on the world.

Objective:

To empower and support the next generation of entrepreneurs by providing a platform for kid-led businesses, mentorship, peer-to-peer learning opportunities, and access to resources for academic and extracurricular development.

Goals

  • To establish a successful and profitable marketplace for kid-led businesses.
  • To provide mentorship and resources for kid entrepreneurs to help them succeed.
  • To create a platform for peer-to-peer learning, where kids can teach and learn from each other in a variety of subjects and activities.
  • To provide coaching and resources for students preparing for competitive exams
  • To raise funds for student-led startups
  • To create a platform for student freelancers to connect with potential clients and grow their businesses.

E-Commerce

Products

Flagship Marketplace allows students to buy and sell a wide range of products, from mobile phone covers to clothing. The platform also promotes sustainability by encouraging and educating students to develop eco-friendly products.

P2P Learning + High End Services

Students on Flagship platform can offer different services as well. Students can offer lessons on topics such as gaming, guitar, piano etc or they can freelance and write content or do graphic designing or SEO.

HyperMarket

The platform encourages students to use their talents and skills to create something unique and valuable that can be sold to others. This gives them an opportunity to showcase their culinary skills and earn some income from it.



Learning Management

Entrepreneurship

"Entrepreneurship 101" course is taught by industry experts, providing students with hands-on experience and practical knowledge to develop their own startup ideas and launch successful businesses.

3P Tutors

"Peer-to-Peer Tutoring" matches students with other students as tutors for specific subjects, providing a flexible and personalized learning experience. It helps students develop leadership, management skills, & improves academic performance, & creates a sense of community among students.

Competitive Exam Preparation

"SAT Preparation" course is designed to help students succeed on the standardized test. Led by experienced teachers, the course covers all sections of the test. The course also provides students with test strategies, practice materials, regular assessments and feedback.



School Outreach

Online Entrepreneurship curated for schools

An "Online Entrepreneurship Bootcamp" for schools provides students with an immersive, hands-on experience in developing and launching their own businesses. Led by industry experts, it has a mix of interactive workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

On-Campus Entrepreneurship curated for schools

On-Campus entrepreneurship camp for students to learn and get a deeper understanding of entrepreneurship along with students to connect and learn from their peers as well.

Curated specifically for schools

The curriculum is tailored to be age-appropriate and is aimed at inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs. This program provides students a unique learning opportunity for real-world practical skills.
